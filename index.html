Singer, Songwriter and Radio Personality
Overview

Richard Paul Astley (born 6 February 1966) is an English singer who has been active in music for several decades. He gained worldwide fame in the 1980s. Astley became an Internet phenomenon in 2007 when the music video for his song "Never Gonna Give You Up" became integral to the Rickrolling meme, and his performance career was revitalised by the meme's popularity. (Credit: Wikipedia)

Professional History

  • 1985–1986: Signing with Stock Aitken Waterman
  • 1987–1989: Success
  • 1990–1993: Switch to soul and adult contemporary and career break
  • 1994–2000: Retirement
  • 2000–present: Return to singing and renewed success
